About Brno Blend

Welcome to Brno Blend, your personalized guide to discovering events in Brno!

Motivation

Brno, with its vibrant cultural scene and diverse events, offers a plethora of activities for its residents and visitors. However, navigating through this rich tapestry of events can be overwhelming. Many people struggle to find events that match their interests, leading to missed opportunities and frustration.

The motivation behind Brno Blend is to simplify this process by leveraging the power of data and personal preferences. By harnessing data from data.brno.cz and using intelligent algorithms, Brno Blend aims to deliver personalized event recommendations tailored to your unique preferences.

What is Brno Blend?

Brno Blend is a web application designed to help users discover and explore events in Brno that match their interests. By leveraging data from data.brno.cz and using intelligent algorithms, Brno Blend curates a tailored list of events for each user, enhancing their event discovery experience.

How It Works

  1. Personalized Preferences: Users specify their favorite event categories, preferred locations, and desired dates.
  2. Smart Recommendations: Brno Blend analyzes user preferences and matches them with relevant events from its database.
  3. Explore and Enjoy: Users can view detailed event information, including descriptions, dates, and locations, and decide which events to attend.
